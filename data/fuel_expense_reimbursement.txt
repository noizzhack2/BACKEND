Fuel Expense Reimbursement Form

Purpose:
This form is used to request reimbursement for fuel expenses incurred during business travel or authorized vehicle use.

Instructions:
1. Fill in all required fields marked with an asterisk (*)
2. Attach receipts or proof of fuel purchase
3. Submit within 30 days of the expense
4. Manager approval required before processing

Form Fields:

1. Employee Information
   - Employee Name (required)
   - Employee ID (required)
   - Department (required)
   - Email Address (required)
   - Phone Number (required)

2. Expense Details
   - Date of Fuel Purchase (required) - Format: MM/DD/YYYY
   - Fuel Station Name (required)
   - Location/City (required)
   - Amount in Gallons (required) - Numeric value
   - Price per Gallon (required) - Currency format
   - Total Amount (required) - Auto-calculated
   - Vehicle Registration Number (required)
   - Starting Odometer Reading (required) - Numeric
   - Ending Odometer Reading (required) - Numeric
   - Total Miles Driven (required) - Auto-calculated

3. Trip Purpose
   - Trip Destination (required)
   - Trip Purpose (required) - Select from: Business Meeting, Client Visit, Conference, Training, Other
   - Business Justification (required) - Text area, minimum 50 characters
   - Department Code (required)
   - Project Code (optional)

4. Approval
   - Manager Name (required)
   - Manager Email (required)
   - Approval Status - Read-only field
   - Approval Date - Read-only field

5. Supporting Documentation
   - Receipt Upload (required) - Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
   - Additional Notes (optional) - Text area

6. Declaration
   - I certify that this expense is accurate and business-related (required) - Checkbox
   - I have attached the original receipt (required) - Checkbox

Notes:
- Reimbursement will be processed within 5-7 business days
- Personal vehicle use must be pre-approved
- Corporate credit card should be used when available
- Fuel prices must be market rate
