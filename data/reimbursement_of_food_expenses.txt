Reimbursement of Food Expenses Form

Purpose:
This form is used to request reimbursement for meal and food expenses incurred during business travel, client entertainment, or team events.

Instructions:
1. Complete all required fields marked with an asterisk (*)
2. Attach itemized receipts for each meal
3. Submit within 30 days of the expense
4. Expenses must comply with company travel policy
5. Obtain manager approval before submission

Form Fields:

1. Employee Information
   - Employee Name (required)
   - Employee ID (required)
   - Department (required)
   - Email Address (required)
   - Phone Number (required)
   - Cost Center (required)

2. Expense Summary
   - Expense Report Period (required) - Date range picker
   - Report Submission Date (required)
   - Currency (required) - Select: USD, EUR, GBP, Other
   - Total Meal Expenses (required) - Auto-calculated

3. Meal Details (Multiple entries allowed)
   For each meal:
   - Date of Meal (required) - Format: MM/DD/YYYY
   - Restaurant/Vendor Name (required)
   - Location/City (required)
   - Meal Type (required) - Select: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks
   - Business Purpose (required) - Select: Business Meeting, Client Entertainment, Team Event, Conference, Training, Travel
   - Attendees Count (required) - Numeric value (minimum 1)
   - Attendee Names/Details (required) - Text area
   - Meal Amount (required) - Currency format
   - Tip Amount (optional) - Currency format
   - Tax Amount (required) - Currency format
   - Total for This Meal (required) - Auto-calculated

4. Attendee Details
   - Company Attendees (required) - Employee names
   - Client/External Attendees (required) - Name and company
   - Business Discussion Topics (required) - Text area
   - Expected Outcomes/Follow-ups (required) - Text area

5. Compliance
   - All attendees are company employees or clients (required) - Checkbox
   - Expenses comply with company policy (required) - Checkbox
   - Per Diem was not claimed for the same day (required) - Checkbox
   - Duplicate reimbursement claim has not been submitted (required) - Checkbox

6. Approvals
   - Immediate Manager Name (required)
   - Manager Email (required)
   - Department Head Name (optional) - If amount exceeds threshold
   - Approval Date - Read-only
   - Approval Status - Read-only

7. Supporting Documentation
   - Receipt Attachments (required) - Multiple files allowed
   - Receipt Format Accepted - PDF, JPG, PNG, TIFF
   - Credit Card Statement (optional) - If applicable
   - Additional Documentation (optional)

8. Additional Information
   - Special Circumstances (optional) - Text area
   - Client Name/Company (optional)
   - Project Code (optional)
   - Funding Source (required) - Select: Department Budget, Project Budget, Client Recharge, Other

9. Verification
   - I confirm all information is accurate and truthful (required) - Checkbox
   - I have retained copies of all receipts (required) - Checkbox
   - All expenses are legitimate business expenses (required) - Checkbox

Notes:
- Standard meal limits per person: Breakfast $15, Lunch $25, Dinner $50
- Alcohol limited to 20% of meal cost
- Reimbursement processed within 5-10 business days
- Missing itemized receipts may delay processing
- Non-compliant expenses may be denied
- Original receipts must show itemization, not just totals
